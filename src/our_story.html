Coffee for
the Soul

Soul Coffee was founded by Rinosh Nasar in 2014, to be an ethical coffee brand. Our coffee is sourced from smallholder farmers in the hill country regions of Sri Lanka. Each bean that goes into the making of Soul Coffee meets our exceptional high standard for what creates a perfectly balanced cup of coffee.

“There is a very obvious need in the local market for a product like this. If you want good fresh coffee you need to either buy your own coffee machine or spend some time to prepare and filter the coffee."
